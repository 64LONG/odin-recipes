Tuna & Cucumber Salad

A cheap,delcious appitizer to compliment your dinner as you wait for it to cook.

ingredients

  • 2 mini cucumbers to be diced
  • 1 packet of Tuna
  • 1/8 cup of diced red onion
  • 1 - 2 Tbsp of your preferred dressing

    • Steps

    1. Dice the cucumbers and set aside
    2. Dice the red onion and set aside
    3. Open a picket of tuna and set aside
    4. Mix all of the ingredients together in a bowl
    5. Top or mix the dressing onto the salad
    6. Enjoy your salad while dinner awaits

Protien-Pudding

This pudding is made with cassin to allow it to be thickened to be topped or layered with cearel or your favorite toppings of your choice.

ingredients

  • 1 scoop of Levels Casein protein
  • 8 Fl oz of a milk of your choice
  • 1/4 - 1/2 Cup of cereal or fruit of your choice to layer or top the pudding with

    • Steps

    1. Pour the milk of your choice into a bow or cup or protien shaker bottle
    2. Add the scoop of caesin protein on top of your milk
    3. Shake the protien shaker bottle or stir with a fork to whisk the milk and powder together
    4. Once the powder and milk are mixed together, then let the mixture sit in a cool space (i.e., refirigerator)
    5. Check every five minutes until the pudding is as thick as you like.
    6. Layer or the top the pudding with your cereal or fruit
    7. Enjoy your newfound pudding and protien intake

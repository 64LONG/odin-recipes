Sparkling Cherry Limeade Vodka

This drink is a refreshing way to look after a hard day after work with large amounts of sugar and calories.

ingredients

  • 1 shot of vodka
  • 6 Fl oz of cherry or cherry lime flavored sparkling water
  • A pinch of mint for granish
  • A slice or twist of lime for granish
  • optional, lime juice if using the cherry flavored sparkling water
  • optional, ice ("on the rocks") if needing an immediate chill

    • Steps

    1. Pour the shot of vodka into a glass
    2. Add the sparkling water to the glass
    3. Add mint granish to the drink
    4. stir the ingredients together
    5. Add the granish or slice of lime to the top of the glass or on the surface of the drink
    6. Enjoy your refreshing drink
